The 51% rule

The 51% rule is a low-risk, high win-rate, easy-to-run swing framework.

On a big up day that prints a swing high with volume, smart money is likely involved. The 51% retracement of that rally candle approximates their cost zone.

Key points

  • If price holds above the lower 51 line for days after the spike — bias long
  • If a bounce after a drop fails below the upper 51 line — bias short
  • Avoid weak, low-strength names
  • Prefer high strength + volume
